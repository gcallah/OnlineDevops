Why Incremental Development?

  • Small batches of work make testing easier.
  • Easier to find a bug when you have one.
  • Enables rapid feedback from the users on whether the software is delivering what they want.
  • Allows us to deliver value to the user more rapidly.
  • Small batches are more satisfying to the developers, because their software gets used soon after they write it.
Completing and delivering small batches of work at a time is such an important part of DevOps that we could almost say that every other part of DevOps exists to enable frequent, reliable delivery of small improvements to a piece of software.
